John Irwin School of Excellence Award Winner

The Colorado Department of Education has announced that for the 12th consecutive year Monument Academy has been named a John Irwin School of Excellence Award Winner.

This prestigious award is given to the top 8% of Colorado Public Schools. Schools that demonstrate excellent academic achievement exceeding expectations on the states academic achievement indicator are recognized.

Welcome to Uplift Heights Prep

We are a free, public, college-preparatory, charter school located in west Dallas, and serving grades K-11. Uplift Heights Middle School is an International Baccalaureate World School, authorized in the Middle Years Programme. Click here to learn more. We are part of Uplift Education which is the largest charter school network in North Texas. Learn more by exploring the About Our School pages.

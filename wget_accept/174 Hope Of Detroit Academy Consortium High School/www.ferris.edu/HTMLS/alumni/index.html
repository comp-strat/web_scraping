Colorado Avalanche Alumni Event

Denver - March 15, 2017

Join the Mile High Bulldogs as they host the Colorado Avalanche vs. the Detroit Red Wings! With multiple seating options available, mix and mingle at the pre-game reception with your Colorado-based Ferris family!

Detroit Pistons VIP Experience

Detroit - March 17, 2017


Ever wish to take in a professional sports event in the lap of luxury? Join us at this fun, all-inclusive, event with "Suite styled" seating, all-you-can-eat buffet and great competition as the Pistons face the Toronto Raptors!

Alumni Association Golf Outing

Frankenmuth - May 12, 2017

Join the Alumni Association as they kick-off the summer schedule of events with the "Annual Alumni Golf Outing." Test your luck on the smooth greens of "The Fortress" (Frankenmuth) and enjoy the day with your Ferris family!

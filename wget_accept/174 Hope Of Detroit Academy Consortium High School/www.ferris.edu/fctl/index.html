Faculty Center for Teaching and Learning

The Faculty Center for Teaching and Learning’s (FCTL) aim is to support all who teach at and for Ferris State University.  We do this – individually and collaboratively – by responding to and meeting the needs and interests of individual faculty, academic units, and the broader University community.  In addition to its one-on-one support and assistance for faculty, FCTL’s programs include the New Faculty Transition Program, Junior Faculty Fellows Program, Course Design Institute, Academic Service-Learning Program, Political Engagement Project, Economic Inequality Initiative, various semester- and year-long faculty learning communities, and frequent workshops, books discussion groups, and technology training sessions.

Course Design Institute (CDI) applications are due Friday, April 7.  Please see our newsletter for more information.

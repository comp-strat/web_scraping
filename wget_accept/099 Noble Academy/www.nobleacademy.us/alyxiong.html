Aly Xiong

alyxiong@nobleacademy.us

 

Education:

Bachelors and Masters in Social Work

 

Experience:

My very first hands-on experience with children started during my 2 years of service with AmeriCorps, where I got the privilege of working with a vast diverse student population including, African American students, Hispanic, Hmong, White, and children with disabilities. Through my AmeriCorps experience and after school programming positions, I strived to enhance youth academic achievement through tutoring, social support, youth development, and community service.

 

Reason for becoming a Teacher:

Children are curious creatures and it is our job as educators to nurture that curiosity and encourage creativity to help them discover their potentials. I studied social work but wanted my focus to be in a school setting because every child should be given Â access to quality education. Education is knowledge and knowledge is power. It is the youths of today who will make a difference tomorrow. We must invest time and energy to help them reach their fullest potentials. This is why I so passionately want to work in our education systems, to better the outcomes of our youths. I want to be able to help the children in my community become successful learners and graduates.

 

Interesting Facts and Hobbies:

I LOVE SOCCER! EAT, SLEEP, BREATHE SOCCER ALL DAY, EVERYDAY if I could! Besides soccer, I like to keep myself active by running 10ks and half marathons, join a weight/strength training class, or spend time with my friends and family.
